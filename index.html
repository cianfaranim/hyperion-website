Who we are

More than a
trucking sim.

Hyperion Logistics is a Virtual Trucking Company built around community, consistency, and the open road. We run convoys, track careers, celebrate milestones, and grow together as drivers across ATS and ETS2.

Whether you're hauling solo or rolling with the crew on a convoy, Hyperion gives you the tools to track every kilometre, earn XP, unlock achievements, and climb the ranks.
